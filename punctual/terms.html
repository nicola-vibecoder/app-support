What the App Does

Punctual schedules alarms before your meetings so they ring even when your iPhone is in Silent or Focus mode. You enter meeting times manually; the app does not access your calendar.

Subscription

Punctual is free to try for 7 days. After the trial, continued use requires an active subscription:

  • Monthly: billed monthly.
  • Annual: billed once per year.

Subscriptions are processed by Apple's App Store. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your iPhone Settings under your Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Free Trial

A 7-day free trial is available to new subscribers only. If you do not subscribe before the trial ends, access to scheduling new alarms will be paused until a subscription is active.
