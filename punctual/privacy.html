Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

Punctual is designed with privacy as a default. This policy explains what data the app handles and what it does not.

Data We Do Not Collect

Punctual does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not access your calendar, contacts, photos, location, or any other personal data on your device.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Your scheduled meeting times and the alarms associated with them.
  • Your default alarm settings (number of alarms, offsets, alert mode).

This data is automatically removed when alarms expire or when you uninstall the app.

System Permissions

Punctual requests permission to schedule alarms through Apple's AlarmKit framework. This permission is required for the app to function. Alarms are managed by iOS and never sent to any external server.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Punctual, your purchase is processed by Apple's App Store. The app uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives anonymized purchase data (a randomly generated user identifier, your subscription status, country, and device type) so that your subscription works correctly across reinstalls and devices. RevenueCat does not receive any personally identifying information. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Analytics and Tracking

Punctual does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Punctual is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be raised by opening an issue at github.com/nicola-vibecoder/punctual/issues.
